Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026 · Version 1.0
SikJipSa (hereinafter the “Service”) treats users’ personal information with care and complies with applicable laws, including the Personal Information Protection Act. This Policy explains what information the Service collects, for what purposes it is collected, used, and stored, and what rights users have.

1. Categories of Personal Information Collected

CategoryItemsMethod of Collection
Account information Email address, nickname (display name), social login identifier, sign-up provider (Kakao / Google) Provided with the user’s consent upon social login via Kakao or Google
User content Registered plant information, care records (watering, repotting, fertilizing, etc.), notes, photos Entered and uploaded by the user directly into the Service
Location information Device GPS coordinates or approximate city-level location based on IP address Collected only where the user has consented for “weather-based recommendations”

The Service does not collect unique identifying information such as resident registration numbers, nor any payment information.

2. Purposes of Collection and Use of Personal Information

  • Identifying members, maintaining login sessions, and syncing the user’s own data across devices
  • Managing plant care schedules and storing care records and photos
  • Providing location-based weather information and watering recommendations
  • Sending schedule notifications, such as watering reminders
  • Operating the Service, responding to errors, and improving quality

3. Retention and Use Period

  1. The user’s personal information is retained and used until the user withdraws membership (deletes their account).
  2. When a user performs “Delete Account” within the Service, their account information and all registered content (plants, records, photos) are permanently deleted without delay and cannot be recovered.
  3. Where applicable laws require retention, the information is stored for the relevant period and then destroyed.

4. Provision to Third Parties and Outsourcing of Processing

The Service does not sell users’ personal information to third parties or provide it for marketing purposes. However, in order to provide the Service, it uses the following external services, and the minimum information necessary may be transmitted in the course of processing.

5. Use of Location Information

Location information is used solely for retrieving weather information and providing watering recommendations, and is not tracked in the form of a separately stored movement history or the like. Users may decline the use of location through their device settings or within the Service; in that case, the accuracy of weather-based recommendations may be reduced.

6. Notifications (Push)

The Service uses device notifications to provide reminders for care schedules, such as watering. Users can directly manage whether they receive notifications and at what time on their device settings or on the “Me” screen within the Service.

7. Users’ Rights and How to Exercise Them

  • Users may view and correct their own personal information at any time. (Plants and records can be edited within the Service; nicknames are managed by the social login provider.)
  • Users may withdraw their consent to the processing of personal information and delete all of their data through “Delete Account.”
  • Inquiries about personal information and requests to access or delete it may be made to the contact below, and they will be handled without delay in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Measures to Ensure the Security of Personal Information

To handle personal information securely, the Service applies protective measures such as encryption of data in transit (HTTPS), access privilege management, and access controls that allow only authenticated users to access their own data.

9. Children Under the Age of 14

The Service is not primarily intended for children under the age of 14 and does not knowingly collect the personal information of children under the age of 14.
